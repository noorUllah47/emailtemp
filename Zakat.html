Zakat Exemption

You mentioned that you wish to be exempted from Zakat deductions on dividend income. Regulatory authorities require us to submit a signed declaration from your end to be exempted. The exemption will only apply once the declaration is submitted by you.

Instructions to submit a Zakat declaration:

  1. Print out the declaration attached on print paper (stamp paper is not required)
  2. Fill and sign it
  3. Notarize the document
  4. Create a digital copy of the declaration form by scanning it or by using CamScanner or a similar application
  5. Respond back to this email with the digital copy of the filled declaration form

An example digital copy has been attached for your ease.

Note that Zakat deductions deduct 2.5% from dividend payouts. Zakat deductions are not applicable on any other gains.
